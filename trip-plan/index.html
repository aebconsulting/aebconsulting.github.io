Recommended Trails

  • Hickory Nut Falls Trail 1.4 mi round trip · Easy/Moderate · Leads to 404-ft waterfall
  • Exclamation Point Trail 1.4 mi round trip · Moderate · Best panoramic views of Lake Lure & Hickory Nut Gorge
  • Four Seasons Trail 0.6 mi · Easy · Nature walk through forest canopy
  • Skyline–Cliff Trail Loop 0.4 mi · Easy · Connects Chimney top to Exclamation Point

Looking Glass Falls

Afternoon Hike — Tuesday, March 17 · Pisgah National Forest, Brevard, NC

Trail Map

Falls Details

Location
US-276, Pisgah Forest
Waterfall Height
60 feet
Trail Distance
~0.2 mi to base
Admission
Free

What to Expect

  • Falls Viewpoint (Roadside) Visible directly from the US-276 pull-off — great for photos
  • Staircase to Base Short paved stairway (about 60 steps) descends to the base of the falls
  • Wading Area Shallow pool at the base — kids can wade in warmer months (water is cold in March!)

Nearby Bonus Stops

  • Sliding Rock 7 min drive · Natural 60-ft rock waterslide (seasonal — may be closed in March)
  • Brevard 15 min drive · Charming downtown with shops, coffee, and ice cream

Suggested Afternoon Schedule

1:30 PM
Depart Chimney Rock Village (~1 hr 15 min drive via US-64 W & US-276)
2:45 PM
Arrive at Looking Glass Falls pull-off
2:50 PM
Walk down staircase to falls base, explore & take photos
3:30 PM
Optional: Drive to Sliding Rock for a quick look
4:00 PM
Head to Brevard for snacks or continue to next destination

Driving Route: Chimney Rock → Looking Glass Falls

Tip: Looking Glass Falls is a quick stop — plan 30–45 minutes. The drive from Chimney Rock is scenic through the mountains. March water levels are usually strong, making the falls impressive. Bring a jacket — the mist is cold!
